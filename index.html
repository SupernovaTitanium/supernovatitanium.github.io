I am a first-year MS/PhD student in Computer Science at KAUST. I am very fortunate to be advised by Prof. Francesco Orabona. Before joining KAUST, I worked with Prof. Chi-Jen Lu at Academia Sinica and Prof. Shou-De Lin at National Taiwan University.

My research interests lies in developing practical optimization algorithms with strong theoretical guarantees, with a focus on online learning and reinforcement learning. I am also interested in statistical learning theory and using computers to assist mathematical research.

Chaotic with a meme, sharp with a proof, fast with a script, and precise with a twist.
